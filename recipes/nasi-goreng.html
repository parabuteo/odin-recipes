Simple Nasi Goreng Recipe

Nasi Goreng (Indonesian Fried Rice)

Nasi Goreng is the popular Indonesian fried rice which is traditionally served with a fried egg.

This version is super easy and will not require you to find any unusual ingredients.

It's an absolute favorite in our household, and I know you'll love it too.

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 5 oz chicken breast
  • 1 Tbsp kecap manis (sweet soy sauce)

Rice

  • 1.5 tbsp oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp red chili, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, small diced
  • 3 cups cooked white rice, day old, cold for best results
  • 2 tbsp kecap manis
  • 2 tsp chrimp paste, optional

Garnishes/Side Servings (Optional)

  • 4 eggs, fried to taste
  • 1 green onion, sliced
  • tomatoes and cucumbers, cut into wedges/chunks
  • lime wedges

Steps for Preparation

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat
  2. Add chili and garlic, stir for 10 seconds
  3. Add onion, cook for one minute
  4. Add chicken, cook until it has turned mostly white, then add 1 tbsp kecap manis and cook for a further 1 minute or until chicken is mostly cooked through and a bit caramelised.
  5. Add rice, 2 tbsp kecap manis and shrimp paste. Cook, stirring constantly for 2 minutes until sauce reduces down and rice grains start to caramelise.
  6. Serve, topped with fried egg, cucumber, and tomato, and garnished with your chosen garnishes (green onion, red chili, fried shallots)
